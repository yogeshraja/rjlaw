We strive to enable legal consultation for all of the public regardless of their status and class

A little something about us

RJ has a team of Well educated and a team of Advocates, Consultants and Experts of the respective field. This ensures that trusted legal advice to clients are given in the right time, on simple as well as in complex matters in their litigation.

Our legal services are free for people in Below Poverty Line (BPL) in the areas of Free Legal Consultation and Free Legal Aid on Litigation. The primary objective of this website is to ensure that people search online for a reputed and trustworthy Law Firm and reach us for getting free legal services. RJ Law Affiliate ensures that they are rightly guided in achieving Justice and the nobility of this profession is met.

Our Aim is to evolve into a world class Muti-Disciplinary Law firm while keeping up with the professional Ethics, Trust, Nobility, Morality and Integrity of the values imbibed amongst the advocates of our Firm and to grow Affiliates with Specalisation in respective areas.
